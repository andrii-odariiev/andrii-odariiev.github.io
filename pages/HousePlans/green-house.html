What difference can design make?

Green Design is more than insulation and energy efficient appliances All of the House Plans on this site are optimized for energy efficiency. Windows are sized with an eye for efficiency and stratigically placed so that glazing is not more than it needs to be. The ratio of wall/ window is ideal for eco friendly construction. Houses are also designed with a maximum ratio of exterior wall space to finished interior sq. ft. Combined, these design qualities provide builders with the foundation they need to build energy effecient houses.
